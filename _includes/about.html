About

Filmmaker enthusiast originally from Monaco now studying Computer Science at the University of Bath (UK). I discovered the magic of filmmaking at the age of 10, and it has since then been a hobby that I enjoyed sharing with friends through social networks, mainly through my YouTube channel.

Starting with my father's camera, I learned to use new gear throughout the years, now mainly using a Canon Legria HV40 and a Nikon D7000, as well as software such as Final Cut Pro X, Motion 5, Adobe After Effects and Adobe Photoshop.
